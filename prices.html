Prices

Here at Second-Hand Sticks we run a unique pricing system that you will see below. The brand of the club does not matter since all of them sell, for relatively the same price.

The two things that are considered when buying or exchanging new clubs are the condition and the age of the club.

Below is the table to better understand the pricing system.
